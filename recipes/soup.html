Description

Soup is a hearty, comforting dish made with a flavorful broth, tender meat, and a mix of fresh vegetables. It’s a wholesome blend of simplicity and nourishment, perfect for cold days or family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Beef or chicken broth – forms the flavorful base
  • Carrots – adds natural sweetness and texture
  • Potatoes – for heartiness and thickness
  • Celery – brings aroma and crunch
  • Onion – essential for deep, savory flavor

Steps

  1. Sauté the aromatics – In a large pot, heat oil and cook chopped onions, garlic, and celery until fragrant.
  2. Add vegetables – Stir in carrots, potatoes, and any other veggies you like.
  3. Pour in broth – Add beef or chicken broth, season with herbs, salt, and pepper, then bring to a boil.
  4. Simmer and serve – Reduce heat and let it simmer for 25–30 minutes until everything is tender. Serve warm.
