Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta made from durum wheat, known for its long, thin, cylindrical shape. It’s commonly served with rich tomato-based sauces, meatballs, or creamy toppings.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Tomato sauce (or crushed tomatoes)
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta – Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente, then drain.
  2. Sauté garlic – In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add sauce – Pour in tomato sauce and let it simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  4. Combine and serve – Toss the cooked spaghetti in the , mix well, and serve hot.
