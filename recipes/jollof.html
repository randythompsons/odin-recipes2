Description

Jollof Rice is a vibrant West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and a rich blend of spices. It's known for its bold, smoky flavor and bright red color, often served with chicken, beef, or fried plantains.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long grain rice (preferably jasmine or basmati)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced (half blended, half for cooking)
  • 3 large tomatoes, chopped (or 1 can of chopped tomatoes)
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 2 red bell peppers, chopped

Steps

  1. Blend the Base
  2. Prepare the Stew
  3. Add Blended Mixture
  4. Season the Stew
